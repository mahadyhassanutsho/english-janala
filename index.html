Frequently Asked Questions

1. How can I start learning English on this website?
You can start by exploring our beginner lessons, interactive exercises, and quizzes. We also offer structured courses to guide you step by step.
2. Is this website free to use?
Yes, this website is completely free to use. You can access all lessons, exercises, and quizzes without any cost.
3. Do I need to create an account?
You are not required to create one, but creating an account allows you to track your progress, save your favorite lessons, and access additional features.
4. How can I build my English vocabulary?
You can build your vocabulary by regularly practicing with our vocabulary lessons, flashcards, and quizzes. We also recommend reading English books and articles to expose yourself to new words.
5. Do you offer certificates for completed course?
Yes, we offer certificates for completing certain courses. You can find more information about this on the course completion page.
